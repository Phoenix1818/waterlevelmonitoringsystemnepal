How Our Technology Works

Our technology uses advanced ultrasonic sensors to measure the water level directly through your smartphone. It's designed to be reliable, easy to use, and affordable, providing real-time data and peace of mind for managing your water supply efficiently.

Step 1: Installation

Our ultrasonic sensors are easily installed on top of your water tank, offering a hassle-free setup process.

Step 2: Real-Time Monitoring

The sensors send accurate water level data to your smartphone, allowing you to monitor the water level anytime, anywhere.

Step 3: Notifications

Get instant notifications when the water level is too high or low, ensuring you never run out or overflow your tank.
